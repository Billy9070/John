Residing in Canberra, Australia, has had a profound impact on both myself and my wife, Lungten Zangmo. The dynamic ethnic atmosphere and the city's unwavering dedication to sustainability strongly align with my personal principles. As a Buddhist, I derive tranquility from the serene moments I spend in nature, be it cycling along picturesque trails, swimming in the pristine waters of Lake Burley Griffin, or trekking through the challenging landscapes of the Australian wilderness. Engaging in these activities not only enhances my physical fitness but also cultivates a sense of equilibrium and harmony in my life.
